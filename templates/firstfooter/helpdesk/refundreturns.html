Refund & Returns Policy

This Refund & Returns Policy applies to all purchases made through the OneBuild Construction & Materials Platform, owned and managed by Speshway Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

Address: T-Hub, 6th Floor, Hyderabad, Telangana, India.

1. Eligible Categories for Returns

Returns are accepted only if the product received is damaged, defective, incorrect, or missing.

  • Aggregates
  • Bricks & Blocks
  • Cement
  • Sand
  • TMT Bars
  • Roofing Materials
  • Windows & Doors
  • Plumbing Items
  • Electrical Items
  • Paints
  • Flooring Materials
  • Interior Products
  • Glass & Glazing Items
  • Insulation Materials
  • Sanitaryware

Note: Not all items can be returned due to logistics and safety conditions.

2. Conditions for Returns

  • Return request must be raised within 24–48 hours.
  • Product must be unused and in original packaging.
  • Invoice / Order ID required.
  • No return on cut-size or custom-made items.

3. How to Request a Return

  • Go to Order Tracking → “Request Return”
  • Email: support@onebuild.in
  • Call / WhatsApp support numbers on our website

4. Refund Process

  • UPI refunds: 2–4 days
  • Bank Transfer: 3–7 days
  • Credit / Debit Card: As per bank process

5. Non-Refundable Items

  • Custom-made or cut-size items
  • Opened cement bags
  • Sand / Aggregates after unloading
  • Installed electrical / plumbing items
