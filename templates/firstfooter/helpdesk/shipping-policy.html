Shipping Policy

ONE-BUILD — Speshway Solutions Pvt. Ltd
Last updated: Loading…

This Shipping Policy describes how OneBuild processes, ships and delivers orders for construction materials, supplies and services booked through our site. For bulky goods, contractor services, or vendor deliveries, special handling and lead times apply — read below for details.

1. Order Processing

Orders received on working days are normally processed within 1–2 business days. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next working day.

  • Payment must be verified before dispatch.
  • If items are out of stock, we will notify you with expected availability.

2. Delivery Areas & Service Types

We deliver within the serviceable areas listed on the site. Delivery services vary by product category:

  • Standard Delivery — Small items (hand tools, small accessories).
  • Bulky Delivery — Aggregates, cement bags, tiles, blocks; requires truck/lorry arrangement.
  • Contractor/Vendor Delivery — For registered suppliers & contractors delivering to site.

3. Delivery Times & Lead Times

Typical delivery windows (business days from dispatch):

ServiceUrbanSuburban / Rural
Standard Delivery1–3 days2–5 days
Bulky Delivery (truck)2–5 days3–8 days
Contractor/Vendor DeliveryAs scheduled with vendorAs scheduled with vendor

Note: Lead times are estimates and may change due to stock, weather, logistics or local restrictions.

4. Shipping Charges & Free Delivery

Shipping charges depend on product weight, volume and destination. At checkout, you will see shipping cost estimates.

  • Small items: fixed or calculated per order.
  • Bulky items: charged per truck/ton or per-lift depending on distance and handling.
  • Free delivery promotions (if any) will be shown at checkout and are subject to terms.

5. Special Handling & Site Requirements

For bulky deliveries, please ensure:

  • Site accessibility for trucks/vehicles (clear height, turning radius).
  • Availability of personnel to receive and offload materials.
  • Any required permits for entry to gated communities or restricted zones.
If a delivery is delayed or returned due to inaccessible site or lack of offloading support, additional re-delivery charges may apply.

6. Tracking & Notifications

Once dispatched, you will receive:

  • Dispatch confirmation via email / SMS
  • Tracking link for courier or delivery partner (where available)
  • Estimated delivery window and driver contact (for bulky deliveries)

7. Damages, Shortages & Returns

Please inspect items at delivery. For damaged or short deliveries:

  • Record damage/shortage on delivery note and ask driver to sign.
  • Contact OneBuild customer support within 24–48 hours with photos and details.
  • We will arrange replacement or refund per our Refund Policy.

8. Cancellations & Change Requests

  • Orders not yet dispatched can be cancelled (refund processed as per our Refund Policy).
  • Once dispatched, cancellations may be treated as returns and subject to return & logistics charges.
  • To request changes, contact support immediately — we will try to accommodate but cannot guarantee after dispatch.

9. Pricing & Availability

Prices for materials and services may change and are only guaranteed after order confirmation and payment. Stock availability is subject to supplier inventory.

10. Liability & Insurance

We exercise reasonable care in handling and delivery. OneBuild is not liable for:

  • Site damage caused by heavy vehicle movements (unless caused by negligent delivery team)
  • Delays due to force majeure (weather, strikes, natural disasters)

For high-value or sensitive shipments, consider transit insurance — contact us for options.
